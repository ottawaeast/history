Archville Directories: 1877 to 1881

Table Description:
Addresses are listed as they appear in the various directories. House numbers and street names appear in the later years Second St, B St s s 80 = Lived on Second St and later in 1880 on the south side of B St.
Nil 77 = No listing in that year Fifth St (#8 in 80) = House number appears in 1880
ws = west side, ns = north side, etc. Macadamized or New Road = Main St.
77 = 1877, 78 = 1878, etc Clay St. = Possibly part of Main St. at Canal Road
Facing Canal = Canal Rd. (Echo Drive) Section St. = Possibly Canal Rd.
For a complete Street Index - Go here B St. = Second St. (Greenfield Ave.)
