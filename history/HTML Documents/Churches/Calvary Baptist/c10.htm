On April 4, 5, 1948, the Church's 33rd Anniversary and Re-dedication Services were held. A special booklet was prepared at that time and the following message, by the Pastor, was included in it -

THE CHURCH OF GOD

This was a favorite phrase of the Apostle Paul. He wasn't thinking in terms of a material building but of the assembly of born again believers united
as a witness to the saving grace of God's "only begotten Son".

He reminded the idolatrous Athenian that God , "dwelleth not in temples made with hands". And the Lord Jesus Christ said to the Samaritan woman, "God is a Spirit, and they that worship
him must worship him in spirit and in truth".

Material buildings are an earthly, temporal necessity but the spiritual reality of, 'the church of God', is heavenly and eternal.

A true church, then, is not that made "with men's hands", but that spiritual building of which Peter said, "Ye also, as lively stones, are built up a spiritual house, an holy priesthood, to offer up spiritual sacrifices , acceptable to God by Jesus Christ', and, as Paul said, "are built upon the foundation of the apostles and prophets, Jesus Christ himself being the chief corner stone; In whom all the building fitly framed together groweth unto a holy temple in the Lord; In whom ye also are builded together for an habitation of God through the Spirit".
