Minutes of the Board of Trustees, May 5, 1985.
Members of the Pastoral Relations Committee appointed December 9, 1985 were: Peg Irvine,
Margaret Marshall, Joan Smith, Paula Smith, Dorothy Wallace, Paul Fuoco, Len Merrifield,
Wayne Pointen and Gary Simser. Paul Fuoco was chairman and Joan Smith was secretary.
Those conducting services during Rev. McDonald's forced absence were: Rev. Joanne Barr for 9
Sundays, Rev. Manliff Mitchell for 2 Sundays, Frank Hare for 2 Sundays and Dave Patterson, 2
Sundays.
The Lord's House, (psalm 122) was a duet written for Laurine and Wayne Pointen, soprano and
bass singers in Wesley's choir. The performance included Linda Smith and Cyril Chapman play-
ing soprano recorders with organist, Grace Davies at the organ.
