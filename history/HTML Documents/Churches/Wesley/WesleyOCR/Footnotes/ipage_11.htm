Terms of the Evelyn TheIma Merrifield Memorial Fund:
1.
The Fund shall always be known as the Eve/yn Thelma Merrifield Memorial Fund.
Note:(l)
Note (2)
2.
The money in the Fund shall be invested in any investment which the Board of
Trustees deems reasonably secure and likely to return a fair annual income, not
being limited to investments expressly authorized by law. The Board of Trustees
is exonerated from any responsibility for loss or damage by reason of invest-
ments made in good faith in securities other than those authorized by law.
3.
Interest earned by the Fund, less an amount described under provision 5, shall
be given to the Benevolent Fund for charitable work.
4.
The Principal of the Fund is not to be spent nor borrowed.
5.
The present day value of the Fund shall be maintained by adding annually to the
principal of the Fund from interest earned, an amount equal to the current rate of
inflation.
6.
Should the Benevolent Fund cease to exist, the Memorial Fund shall be
transferred to the Ottawa Presbytery, or its equivalent, to be managed as per pro-
visions 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5, and the Memorial Fund earnings used to support work
similar to the Benevolent Fund in the Ottawa area.
7.
Should any of the above provisions be circumvented, the full inflation adjusted
value of the Fund at the time, shall be immediately owed to the Alice Ann and
Evelyn Thelma Merrifield Memorial Fund, managed by Carleton University of
Ottawa.
