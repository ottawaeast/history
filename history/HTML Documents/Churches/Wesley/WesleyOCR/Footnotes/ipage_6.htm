During the thirties and early forties, the Sunday School held picnics at Britannia Park, a large
Ottawa Electric Railway complex, complete with stores, a pavilion and a long pier reaching out
from a sandy beach into the Ottawa River. The complex, located on what was the western limits
of the city in the 1930's, included a large picnic and games area. Later outings were held at
Penguin Park in the Gatineau. Picnics were held even earlier in Rockliffe park with access via a
boat plying the Ottawa River.
Wesley's connection with the Welsh Choir was through Grace and Tom Davies. Tom was a
singer in the organization and Grace was its accompanist for several years. One of our choir
members, Beverley Cleland currently remains a member of the Welsh Choral Society.
