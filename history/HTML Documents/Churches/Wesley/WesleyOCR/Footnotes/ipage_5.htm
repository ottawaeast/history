Minutes of the Official Board, March 1, 1972.
"Celebration"
On this day of celebration
Father, do we gladly come
Hoping, praying all creation
Will Your church soon make its own.
Throughout hours oftribulation,
Master, You have shown the way;
Sing we now in exultation
For fwill never pass away.
Ever onward, ever upward
We have moved toward this day.
You, who are our shield and buckler,
Found us when we went astray.
When all others laughed and mocked us
And would o'er us all prevail,
Like a great and mighty fortress
Stood Your Church that will not fail.
