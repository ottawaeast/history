Among the groups invited to participate in worship services were two handbell choirs from
Rideau Park United Church.. One group, "A Touch of Brass", performed twice: December 12,
1993 and February 18, 1996. Another Rideau Park hand bell choir, "A'Pealing Sound Ringers",
was on hand at the January, 1995 service.
Another group of note visited November 12, 1995. The sacred music of Edward (Duke)
Ellington was sung by Edward Ellington ill and his sister, April. Also performing was the
Heritage College Big Band. Edward and April were invited by their sister, Ava Kelly.
The Session, on March 21, 1995, announced that it had decided not to purchase copies of the
new United Church hymn book Voices United, but would instead use a $100. gift to obtain
licence to photocopy up to 1500 new hymns. (Official Board, March 21,1995) In October of 1995,
the Memorial Committee purchased a new photocopier for the church using funds received
through the Memorial Book program. It replaced a Gestetner duplicator, acquired second hand in
1986. A clip-on microphone was also purchased for use during worship.
