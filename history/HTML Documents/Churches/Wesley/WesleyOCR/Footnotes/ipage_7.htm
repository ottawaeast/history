VII
Lo, the Spirit of the Father has been given us with power,
To redeem our way of living and uphold us every hour;
So we tell the world about Him and we sing of His great dower,
His love is borne in song.
Glory, glory hallelujah., His love is borne in song!
Though the generations pass and history marches through the years,
Though each life is made of happiness, and each a share of tears;
Still our fellowship continues, and His Body perseveres,
His flame is burning on.
Glory, glory hallelujah., His flame is burning on!
He was there in the beginning as Creator, Father, God,
He was there when man first turned his hand to till the virgin sod;
He will be in all our future as a just and loving rod,
He calls us always on.
Glory, glory hallelujah., He calls us always on!
So we join this Wesley fellowship with others of the faith,
All who call upon the name of Him, the Lord of all the earth;
With our hearts and hands, together we will build a life of worth,
He leads us always on.
Glory, glory hallelujah., He leads us always on!
C 1983, D.R. McDonald. Used by permission.

Hymn: We Have This Faith by Rev. D.R. McDonald. First sung October 28, 1984
(tune: Sine Nomine)
We have this faith, a heritage divine,
Which came from distant lands across the sea,
And now we join to claim ourselves as thine:
Alleluia! Alleluia!
He walked this earth, and fished on Galilee,
He healed the sick and made the water wine;
The Father's love led him to Calvary:
Alleluia! Alleluia!
A new day dawned when from the grave he rose;
He lives, and calls us to his service free,
That all may know that shared love always grows:
Alleluia! Alleluia!
Now we must go from Anniversary
The world to win, and evil to depose.
Our children soon His Church must learn to be:
Alleluia! Alleluia!
C 1984, D.R. McDonald. Used by permission.
