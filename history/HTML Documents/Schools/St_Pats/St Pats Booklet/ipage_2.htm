PREFACE
The present monograph was completed at the request
of the Canadian Catholic Historical Association for
presentation at its annual meeting in June 1982. The
author regrets that constraints of space made it
necessary to exclude the names of many who
contributed significantly to the development of 8t.
Patrick's College.
NOTE ON THE AUTHOR
Hugh A. MacDougall is currently a professor of
history at Carleton University. His publications
include The Acton-Newman Relations (1962), Lord
Acton on Papal Power (1973), Racial Myth in English
History (1982). Through 1961-67 and 1973-76 he
served as Dean of St. Patrick's College.
