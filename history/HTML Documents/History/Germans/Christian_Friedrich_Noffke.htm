1. CHRISTIAN FRIEDRICH 2 NOFFKE (MICHAEL 1 ) was born September 03, 1815 in Germany 1 , and died June 02, 1902 in 631 Chapel Street, Ottawa 2 . He married WILHELMINE RABITZ.

Notes for CHRISTIAN FRIEDRICH NOFFKE:

Christian Noffke came to Canada on the S.S. Hamburg, departing the port of Hamburg on the 18 April 1884. He was with his daughter Auguste and son-in-law Heinrich Grummisch and their 3 month son Franz. It appears that Christian was probably a widower. Their previous residence is listed as Rotten, which is in Kreis Stolp, Pomerania. It is not known when his son Franz arrived in Canada but the 1901 census suggests it was the following. year.
