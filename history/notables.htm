Notable People in the early history of Ottawa East

In the long history of Ottawa East that began in the early 1800's, many individuals emerged as major contributors to the community. Below is but a brief snapshot of just a few of these important people. It is not intended to be definitive. Time and energy has precluded the inclusion of hundreds of other people who also made a significant contribution to the evolution of Ottawa East. But these biographies will offer a brief glimpse of the intriguing personalities that created our history.
