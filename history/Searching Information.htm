Searching the Collection
Search Now! - Click and away you go!
Or, on the other hand you could read the following suggestions regarding use of the Search Engine.
The entire contents of this CD-ROM have been indexed by text. Following some simple suggestions will find the text and then display all the individual reference in a separate window. In that separate window these references are hotlinked so all you have to do is click on the link to go to the specific page.
Guidelines:
The search engine will find pages on this CD-ROM only.
For best results enter just one or two words. DO NOT use quotes " ".
Searches on more than one word will be treated "as a phrase".
Searches for text are case insensitive - Do not bother with capitals.
Use the asterisk (*) character for wildcard searches.
You cannot search for image file names.
